About Us

Welcome to BankeMo

Welcome!! We are passionate about providing the best quality from our stock of YAM and PLANTAIN FLOUR (Elubo), GARI IJEBU, STONE FREE HONEY BEANS (Ewa Oloyin), DRUM BEANS, STONE FREE OFADA RICE, SNAIL, CRAYFISH, and original HONEY (Oyin Igan).. At our store, we believe that food is more than just sustenance - it's an experience. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction is evident in every item we offer, and we are confident that you will love our food Stuffs as much as we do.

We are dedicated to providing the best quality fabrics to help you bring your fashion dreams to life. Our team of experts carefully selects and sources a wide range of fabrics, from elegant Silks to cozy Cotton,ADIRE and ASO-OKE for your personal needs or Aso-Ebi... to ensure that we have something for every style and occasion. At our cloth fabrics online store, we believe that fashion is a form of self-expression, and we strive to offer fabrics that not only look beautiful but also feel comfortable and luxurious. Whether you're a seasoned designer or a beginner, we are here to provide you with the guidance and support you need to bring your ideas to life. We are committed to providing exceptional customer service and ensuring that you have a smooth and enjoyable shopping experience with us. Our knowledgeable staff is always available to answer any questions you may have and help you find the perfect fabric for your next project.
